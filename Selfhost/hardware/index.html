For MiniPCs

Needs

A PC, Server/NAS, network and a 2K monitor, preferably under 30mil. Mainly a "server" since it can do so much, and minipcs are relatively affordable. Not worth it to buy a gaming PC. After considering, it is better to have a PC and a cheapish minipc using the Intel N150.
